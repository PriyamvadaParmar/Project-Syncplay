Bonus: User Interview Guide

Use this script when interviewing friends/family:

Introduction:

"Hi! I'm working on a project about how people watch movies/shows together. Can I ask you a few questions? It'll take about 10 minutes."

Questions:

  1. How often do you watch Netflix/streaming content? (Daily/Weekly/Monthly)
  2. Do you have friends/family who live far away? Who?
  3. Have you ever wanted to watch something together with someone who wasn't physically with you?
  4. If yes, what did you do? (Called on phone, texted during, used any app?)
  5. What was frustrating about that experience?
  6. If there was a feature that let you watch synchronized with friends, would you use it? Why?
  7. What features would make it better? (Chat, video call, reactions?)
  8. Who would you most want to use this with? (Partner, friends, family?)
  9. How much would you pay for this feature? (Nothing/Small fee/Premium)
  10. Any other thoughts about watching content together remotely?

Closing:

"Thank you so much! This really helps me understand what people need. Can I follow up if I have more questions?"
